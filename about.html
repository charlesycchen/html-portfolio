Hi, I’m Charles Chen

A new BS graduate majored in Physics and minored in Computer Science and Engineering at the National Chung Hsing University.

Had 4-year experience in Python, 1-year experience in Java, C/C++, a little experience in machine learning and image processing with Tensorflow, Scikit-Learn and OpenCV and experience in database management by MongoDB and SQL. Familiar with Unix/Linux operating system.

With the training in physics, always use first principle thinking to get feasible solutions by finding the root causes. Able to express opinions clearly to people from different fields and organize positive ideas to reach a consensus, and good at document writing. Moreover, I have good and strong courage to ask key questions in order to get a big picture of problems. Recently, I just learned project managment skills so as to enhance my personal productivity.

Keen to pursue a career in software engineering with a focus on software development and data science!
